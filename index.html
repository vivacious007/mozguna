About us

Firefox Club JUET GUNAWe are team of awesome volunteers doing awesome things together and supporting Mozilla in its mission by promoting openness, innovation & opportunity on the Web. JUET Firefox Club is working on Mozilla's Mission to promote free , open and innovative web.
Recently our club has been the "Club Of The Month" and has been featured in Mozilla's Monthly newsletter . Moreover club's name along with club members name have been published in Local Newspapers of Guna for its achievements.
Priyam Srivastava's name has been put in credits page of Mozilla.org official website .
